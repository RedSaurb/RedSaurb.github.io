Ranway Village

Ranway is a Village in Keoti Ranway Block in Darbhanga District of Bihar State, India. It comes under Ranway Panchayat. It belongs to Darbhanga Division . It is located 13 KM towards North from District head quarters Darbhanga. 123 KM from State capital Patna Ranway is surrounded by Darbhanga Block towards South , Bisfi Block towards North , Singhwara Block towards west , Bahadurpur Block towards South . Darbhanga , Madhubani , Jhanjharpur , Samastipur are the near by Cities to Ranway. This Place is in the border of the Darbhanga District and Madhubani District. Madhubani District Bisfi is North towards this place.
